Students belonging to any semester, from architecture department can participate in the competition.

Only a single entry is permitted from one college.

A maximum of five students can participate from one college.

All the materials required for the competition will be provided.

Registrations should be made before the closing dates.

All teams are required to submit the concepts of their works before the competition ends.

Registration number would be provided for each team at the time of registrations.


Elimination Criteria

Teams depicting their college identities in any form will be disqualified.

Teams that have not registered will be disqualified.

Use of any materials other than provide is prohibited.

Final decision will be made by the organisers.

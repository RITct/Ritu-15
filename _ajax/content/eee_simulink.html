Simulink Face 2 Face

Have you used Simulink for a student project?

"Simulink Face 2 Face" celebrates the talent, creativity and spirit of students learning with MATLAB and Simulink.

The objective of this contest is to find the talent, creativity and the spirit of students who learn with MATLAB and Simulink by showcasing their innovative and relevant project ideas with simulation.


Event Format :

A team can contain maximum 2 members, individual participants are also allowed.

Participants have to submit their abstract on or before 24/02/2015.

Abstract should contain importance of subject and features of simulation.

Depending on number of entries, selection is based on the evaluation of abstract.

Maximum time allowed for presenting the work with ppt will be 10 minutes.

About Simulation :

The topic for the simulation should be innovative and relevant.

Topic can be selected from any area of Electrical Engineering.

Interactive Simulations are preferred (GUI or VRML).

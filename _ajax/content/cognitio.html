Cognitio - Idea Launch


The purpose of this contest is to recognize an innovative idea, related with technology, chosen from submissions by students and to motivate them to make a giant leap forward – inventing, adapting, and creating bold new ways to make your everyday life easier.

Mail your abstracts to : cognitio@ritu15.com

Judgement Criteria :

Boldness, innovation and originality.

Importance and relevance of the problem addressed and consideration of social needs.

Presentation quality of the submission.

Technical proficiency.

Feasibility and construction of the prototype.

Team size is restricted to maximum of two members.

This contest is open to students from both schools and colleges.

The contestants are required to send abstract to the following mail id and should include team members’ name, contact number, mail id and name of the college.

Mail your abstracts to : cognitio@ritu15.com

Abstract (not more than 200 words) have to be made in pdf/word format.

Selected teams should bring one soft copy and three hard copies of the presentation document on the day of presentation.

Presentation should be in MS-Power point format only.

Time limit for presentation is 10 minutes followed by a question and answer session of 5 minutes.

Last date for mailing your abstract is 21st February, 2015.

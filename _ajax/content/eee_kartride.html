Objective :

The objective of this contest is for a robot to follow a black line on a white background, without losing the line, and navigating several turns.

The robot to complete the course in the shortest period of time while accurately tracking the course line from start to finish wins.


About the Track :

The track width is around 2 cm.

The track contains right angle, acute angles, curves, + sections and may contain white spaces.

Track colour will be of black lines, printed on white surface.

After the game is started , any touch by the participants on the track or robot will result in penalty.


Robot Specs :

Weight should not be above 2.5 kg

Robot dimensions should be below 25 cm x 25 cm x 25 cm.

And yup , the robot should be autonomous.

Participants will not be allowed to use readymade kits.

Participants can use readymade micro controller boards, line sensor array etc.

If a participant uses self-made micro-controller board, sensor array etc., then bonus points will be given. Bonus points will be given to design also.

External power source will not be given and only DC batteries below 24V are allowed.

Battery should be within the robot.

Rules are subjected to change as per decision of organizers.


Event Format :

A team can contain maximum 4 members, individual participants are also allowed.

Depending on number of participants, event may contain 2 rounds.

The track contains right angle, acute angles, curves, + sections and may contain white spaces.

Maximum time allowed for completing a track will be 20 minutes.

Both check points and time taken will be considered to select the winner.

If two teams complete the same number of check points, then team which took minimum time will be the winner.

Ambient lighting condition will be given, any way participants cannot complain about lighting conditions.

If a bot wanders off the track, participants can reset it from the last cleared check point, no extra time will be given for this, and only 3 resets are allowed, points will be deducted for this.

Robots causing any damage to the arena will be disqualified.

Electrical CAD Contest

Electrical cad contest is a competition for B.tech electrical students. It aims to show the knowledge of students in electrical AutoCAD. The competition is designed in two stages.


Objectives :

To test the knowledge of students in basic electrical.

To test the knowledge of students in electrical AutoCAD.

To test the capability of students to manage time efficiently.


Judging Criteria

Knowledge about AutoCAD electrical drawing.

Neatness and time management.



Stage 1 - 30 Min.

The participants will be given a set of questions based on both electrical and AutoCAD. Based on its result screening will be done for the next level.


Stage 2 - 2 Hr.

The screened participants will be given two questions which they have to draw in AutoCAD software. On completion of the event the winners will be decided.



Stage 1

There will be 30 objective questions of which 15 questions will be about AutoCAD electrical drawing and the next 15 questions will be about basics of electrical engineering. The answers should be one word.

Stage 2

There will 2 questions given to those students who passed stage 1.

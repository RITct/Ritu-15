Requirements & Specifications :

Participants are required to bring a self-designed coil gun that operates on a provided 30V DC supply.

Weight of gun = 2 kg

Candidates should bring bullets made of ferromagnetic material with weight less than 20 Gms.

A regulated DC supply (30V) will be provided. No external sources or batteries are allowed.

Use of ICs other than 555 timers is not permitted.

Use of IR photo transistors are permitted.

Readymade purchased circuits are not allowed.


Round 1 - Aptitude Test

The students will be given objective type questions based on 8051 architecture, programming etc.

30 Questions – 20 Minutes

Maximum qualifiers – 10


Round 2 - Testing

In this round the students can display the performance of the gun they have bring for the competition and they can participate if the above conditions are satisfied.

Certificates and Prize Policy :

Certificate of appreciation/participation will be awarded to the all teams. And certificate of excellence will be given to top two teams.

Disqualified teams will not be considered for any certificates.

Rules for the Event :

Each team can have a maximum of 4 members.

Registrations close at 9:30am.

Maximum permitted dimensions are 50cm*50cm*50cm.

The bots can have a maximum weight of 25kg.

The bots must be controlled manually.

The bots can be wired /wireless.

For wired robots, it is recommended to have a minimum length of 4 metres.

Teams should bring their own power supply of maximum 24V( AC supply will be provided at the venue for charging batteries).

Use of extreme ideas like lubricants, chemicals, flames shall not be entertained.

All bots shall be strictly inspected by our volunteers before providing access to the arena.

Slight modification of the bot may be made w.r.t the different stages but modifying the bot to a high extent will not be permitted.

Any team/team member found insulting/disrespecting the rules/opposition teams shall be disqualified.

The decision of the judges will be final and binding.


Round 1

Each bot will have to overcome a simple but rough terrain in order to prove itself worthy of reaching the arena.

In this round there will be a one on one battle where the bot shall show its skills in immobilizing the opponent and defending itself.

Points shall be awarded on the basis of performance.

This will be an 8 min. round.

Round 2

There may be slight changes in the arena.

Rest of the rules are same as that of round 1.

Only four teams with highest points shall be promoted the next round.

Round 3

Will be a 10 min round.

This will be a one on one semi-final knock-out round.

Only 2 survivors shall reach the grand finale.

Round 4

Shall be a 15 min. round.

The last two survivors shall struggle their way out to victory giving all they have.

There shall be slight changes in the arena and rules.

The match shall go on until either one bot has completely immobilized their opponent or the time has elapsed.

If either team has failed to either disfuntion/immobilize the opponents completely, the judges shall decide the winner depending on their respective performance.

Points Rewardal

Crossing opponent terrain - 40 points.

Immobilizing the opponent for 10 secs- 10 points.

Knocking the opponent off the arena - 20 points

Terms

Points shall not be passed on to the next round.

Special point schemes shall be announced at spot.

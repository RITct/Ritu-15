About Us

Acclaimed as one of the best engineering college in the state of Kerala, Govt. Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Technology, has been imparting top-notch technological excellence to students from all over the country for the past 23 years. Spread over a sprawling 90 acres of luscious and picturesque greenery, we have stood by our Vision and crafted an outstanding center for technical excellence on account of the pedagogical superiority of the faculty, the academic excellence of the students, state-of-the-art lab facilities and the distinctive co-curricular subtlety.

The institute is poised to be a temple of science and technology with many of the recent inceptions such as the proposed Ramanujan Institute of Basic Sciences, the fully functional Rapid prototyping facility, Circuit Incubation center and the Soil testing facility are exceptional to this engineering institute. RITU has been conceived to unveil the true grit and shine of our institute, a platform to test and enhance the student community’s fineness. The tech-fest will amalgamate the five B.Tech Departments, the B.Arch. Department and the PG Departments for a fine exertion towards RITU’s triumph.

We are confident that by now you are well-acquainted with the features of RITU and we are profoundly optimistic that you would render us the essentials for accomplishing RITU as a feat of excellence, as a feat that would benchmark the quality of a techno-cultural fest, as a feat with a null possibility for supersedence.

Chapter III.

the Title Sponsor

Rs. 5,00,000/-

  • The festival will be named after the title sponsor i.e. RITU and title sponsor present RITU ’15

  • All posters and banners that form a part of the RITU 15 publicity campaign will carry the name and the logo of the title sponsor.

  • The title sponsor will dominate the hoardings, advertisements and other promotional material of RITU 15.

  • The title sponsor will be given first choice for branding space on the campus during the festival and the precise location will be decided by mutual consent.

  • The title sponsor will get stalls in the festival area for branding purposes. The size and the precise position of the stalls will be decided on by the mutual consent.

  • The title sponsor will be given a slot on the inauguration day to make a presentation of any nature.

  • The RITU website will feature both the title and the profile of the title sponsor with hyperlink to its website. A page on the website shall also be dedicated to the CSR activities of the title sponsor.

  • The title sponsor will also be provided volunteers to help with the publicity efforts of the title sponsor during the course of the festival.

  • Audio and video advertisement of the title sponsor will be shown between the events of RITU 15.

  • Apart from the logistics help provided by our team, we will also ensure that your name reaches the large and prominent RITU alumni.

  • The title sponsor’s name and logo will also be printed on all name badges, certificates and trophies given away in RITU 15.

  • The media partners will publicize the title sponsors name in any article or coverage of RITU15
